What is TheGithubResume?

TheGithubResume is a project being Incubated at SOFTWARE INCUBATOR. This project is about generating your resume easily just by connecting your "GITHUB" account. TheGithubResume reduces the hassle of integrating your projects by time taking process of writing about your projects. TheGithubResume gets all your repositories and adds them to your resume without any effort.

How to use TheGithubResume?

Follow these few clicks to get your resume done:

  • Simply login/register using your email and password.

  • Click On Add User Button.

  • Get your user data by clicking 'Yes'.

  • Click on 'Sync Repositories' for getting your repositories.

  • Push the repositories you want to appear in you resume and Submit.
